## Live runner artifact-binding correction

RC.5 supersedes RC.4 after the guarded live launcher reached the final
pre-mutation artifact-copy gate and failed closed. No InSpace resource was
created. The generic live-run binding compared the verified release revision
with an obsolete `INSPACE_E2E_REVISION` name even though the launcher
intentionally passes `INSPACE_E2E_RELEASE_REVISION`.

The verifier now maps the live binding to
`INSPACE_E2E_RELEASE_REVISION` while retaining
`INSPACE_E2E_BUILT_REVISION` for the independently embedded runner identity.
Static acceptance covers both namespaces and explicitly rejects the obsolete
alias. All image digests, chart bytes, source-tag checks, durable identity
journaling, and fail-closed teardown contracts from RC.4 are unchanged.

Stable promotion remains gated on this exact candidate completing the full
real-cloud lifecycle and restoring the isolated account to its immutable
zero-billable-resource baseline.
